1967 Ford Mustang

The 1967 Ford Mustang is one of the most iconic muscle cars in American automotive history. Known for its aggressive styling and raw performance, it marked the first major redesign of the original Mustang.

Specifications

  • Engine: 4.7L V8 (289 cu in)
  • Horsepower: Up to 271 hp
  • Transmission: 3-speed automatic / 4-speed manual
  • 0–60 mph: Around 6.5 seconds
  • Top Speed: ~120 mph (193 km/h)

Fun Fact

The '67 Mustang was featured in countless movies, most famously in the film Bullitt starring Steve McQueen.
